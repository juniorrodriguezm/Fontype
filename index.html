    Buffon is a reverse-contrast display typeface with a playful personality and a very sassy Heavy weight. With four weights, a generous character set, and ligatures & stylistic alts, Buffon shrugs off the tired Spaghetti Western stereotype so often associated with reverse-contrast—but could make a very nice Wanted poster, if the need arose.

    Diseñador: Dave Coleman
    Aussie type designer & lettering artist. Happiest when working on things that are a bit silly.

    Features


    • 765 glyphs in 18 styles;
    • Extended Latin, Cyrillic and Greek;
    • Geometric forms and low contrast;
    • Prominent x-height which makes it legible in text;
    • Perfect for headlines and logos;
    • Suitable for web, print, motion graphics etc.
    • Semi-condensed proportion;
    • Advanced typographical support and OpenType features including case- sensitive forms, fractions, superscript and subscript characters, and stylistic alternates;
    • Complete set of figures – oldstyle and lining figures, which come with proportional and tabular variation;poster, if the need arose.
